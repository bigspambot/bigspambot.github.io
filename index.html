About Me

Hi, My name is Charlie. I'm from Sheffield in the UK.

I graduated with a BSc in Applied Computing in 2007 but ended up leaving the UK to travel and became an English language teacher abroad for about 5 years and then in England from 2014-2022.

I used to enjoy teaching in classrooms but found back home it was hard to get jobs given my lack of teaching qualifications. I didn't want to have to commit to any more studies and was slowly falling out of love with teaching online.

So I eventually dedided to reignite my interest in computers and began coding in my free time - Codecademy, FreeCodeCamp and Codewars mainly. Before I knew it I was enrolled on a Level 3 Software Dev Bootcamp and enjoying the new challenges that brought.

I'm hoping to become a Junior Web/Software Developer and will be adding to this website to demonstrate my skills and personal projects.
